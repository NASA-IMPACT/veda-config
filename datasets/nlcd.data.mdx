<Prose>
The MRLC's National Land Cover Database (NLCD) land use-land cover classification is derived from Landsat satellite observations over the contiguous United States (CONUS) at 30 meter spatial resolution. Available in two to three-year intervals from 2001-2021, the NLCD classifies the CONUS grid into one of thrity-four land cover classes.
</Prose>
