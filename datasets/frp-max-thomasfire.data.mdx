    ## About
    Maximum Fire Radiative Power recorded by the Suomi NPP VIIRS sensor per 12hr fire line segment for the Thomas Fire of 2017

    ## Scientific Details
    Fire Radiative Power is the rate of emitted radiative energy by the fire at the time of the observations.

    For more information on Fire Radiative Power or NO, please visit the following [NOAA webpage](https://www.star.nesdis.noaa.gov/star/documents/seminardocs/2022/20220420_VAWS_Csiszar_VIIRS_fire.pdf).

    For more information on the satellite Suomi NPP VIIRS, please visit our [NASA webpage](https://ncc.nesdis.noaa.gov/VIIRS/).
